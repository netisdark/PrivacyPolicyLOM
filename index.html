IP Logging and Your Privacy

IP addresses are logged to help us:

  • Secure our platform from unauthorized access or fraud.
  • Debug and resolve technical issues effectively.
  • Comply with legal obligations, if necessary.

However, if a site server detects that the site owner is extracting or logging IP addresses without valid reasons, the following risks may arise:

  • The site owner may lose trust from clients due to privacy concerns.
  • There is a chance that the SSL certificate or site certificate might be compromised or deemed invalid due to a breach of terms.
