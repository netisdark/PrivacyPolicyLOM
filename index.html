Privacy Policy | LABYRINTH OF MINDS

Your Privacy Matters

At LABYRINTH OF MINDS, we are committed to ensuring your data is safe and handled responsibly. This document outlines our practices for data collection, usage, and protection, emphasizing the measures we take to maintain your trust.

IP Logging and Your Privacy

We log IP addresses to:

  • Detect and prevent unauthorized access or fraudulent activities.
  • Identify and resolve technical issues efficiently.
  • Meet compliance requirements with applicable laws and regulations.

To enhance your privacy, we adopt the following practices:

  • Anonymization: Masking parts of IP addresses to protect user identity.
  • Encryption: Securing logs with AES-256 encryption.
  • Retention Policy: Automatically deleting logs after 30 days unless legally required.

Password Security

Your account security is a priority. We ensure the safety of your passwords by implementing the following:

  • Hashed Passwords: All passwords are hashed using advanced algorithms like bcrypt or Argon2, ensuring they are unreadable even in case of a breach.
  • Salted Hashing: Each password is processed with a unique salt to make brute force attacks nearly impossible.
  • Password Strength Enforcement: We encourage strong passwords by requiring a combination of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and special characters.

Advanced Data Protection Practices

We use cutting-edge methods to safeguard your data:

1. Secure File Storage

Sensitive files are encrypted at rest and stored on servers with restricted access.

2. Regular Security Audits

Our systems are audited regularly to detect and patch vulnerabilities before they can be exploited.

3. Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)

Access to our admin panel and critical systems requires MFA, adding an extra layer of security.

SSL Certificate

Your connection to our platform is encrypted using SSL, ensuring the confidentiality of all data transferred between your browser and our servers.

Cookie Policy

Cookies help us enhance your browsing experience. We use:

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for core functionalities like login sessions.
  • Performance Cookies: To monitor and improve site performance.
  • Marketing Cookies: To deliver personalized ads and offers.

You can manage your cookie preferences at any time through your browser settings.

GDPR and CCPA Compliance

We comply with data protection laws, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). You have the right to:

  • Access your data.
  • Request corrections or deletions.
  • Opt-out of data collection or sale.

Contact us if you wish to exercise your rights.
